FAQ

6 pitanja6 questions
Trebam li iskustvo s yogom? Do I need yoga experience?
Ne. Petra radi s početnicima i naprednim praktičarima u istoj grupi. Pravi početnički mindset zapravo je prednost, nema unaprijed naučenih obrazaca koje treba razgraditi. Jedino što trebaš je volja i prisutnost. No. Petra works with beginners and advanced practitioners in the same group. A genuine beginner's mindset is actually an advantage, no pre-learned patterns to undo. All you need is willingness and presence.
Je li hrana biljnog porijekla? Is the food plant-based?
Da, u potpunosti, bez mesa, ribe, jaja i mliječnih proizvoda. Ali nije to estetika šejkova i zelenih sokova. To je prava hrana: darežljiva, začinjena, topla. Salem kuha na retreatima kojima se pridružuje; kad ga nema, Ines vodi kuhinju prema njegovim receptima i pristupu. Ako imaš alergija ili intolerancija, javi nam unaprijed. Yes, fully, no meat, fish, eggs or dairy. But this isn't the aesthetic of shakes and green juices. It's real food: generous, spiced, warm. Salem cooks on retreats he joins; when he's not with us, Ines runs the kitchen following his recipes and approach. If you have allergies or intolerances, let us know in advance.
Mogu li doći sam/sama? Can I come alone?
Da, i većina gostiju dolazi sama. Mala grupa i intiman prostor prirodno stvaraju zajednicu, bez pritiska. Niti jedan retreat do sada nije prošao bez novih poznanstava. Ali nitko se nije morao truditi da ih stvori. Yes, and most guests come alone. The small group and intimate space create community naturally, without pressure. Not one retreat has gone by without new connections forming. But no one has ever had to try.
Kako funkcionira rezervacija i polog? How does booking and the deposit work?
Rezervacija se potvrđuje uplatom pologa od €500, koji se odbija od ukupne cijene retreata. Ostatak od €1.000 plaća se do 22. kolovoza 2026. Polog je povratljiv do 45 dana prije retreata, a u slučaju naše otkaze, vraćamo ga u potpunosti. Booking is confirmed by a €500 deposit, deducted from the total price. The balance €1,000 is due by 22 August 2026. The deposit is refundable up to 45 days before the retreat, and fully refundable in the event of our cancellation.
Što je uključeno u cijenu? What's included in the price?
Smještaj (dijeljeni ili privatni), svi obroci (tri dnevno), sve yoga prakse i materijali. Nije uključeno: prijevoz do lokacije, osobni troškovi, piće. Accommodation (shared or private), all meals (three daily), all yoga practices and materials. Not included: transport to the location, personal expenses, drinks.
Mogu li rezervirati privatnu sobu? Can I book a private room?
Da. Svaki retreat nudi opciju privatne sobe uz doplatu. Broj privatnih soba je ograničen, ako ti je to prioritet, javi nam odmah pri rezervaciji. Yes. Each retreat offers a private room option at an additional cost. The number of private rooms is limited, if it's a priority, let us know immediately when booking.
