Iskustva · Istra · Maroko Experiences · Istria · Morocco

Nije program.
Nije itinerer. Not a schedule.
Not an itinerary.

Ono što doživiš na retreatu teško je opisati jer nije jedno iskustvo, to je zbir trenutaka. Jutarnja tišina. Obrok koji traje sat dulje nego što si planirao/la. Razgovor koji počne slučajno i nikako da završi. Sve to zajedno oblikuje nešto što ne možeš kupiti, samo proživjeti. What you experience on a retreat is hard to describe because it isn't one thing, it's the accumulation of moments. Morning quiet. A meal that lasts an hour longer than planned. A conversation that starts by accident and won't end. All of it together shapes something you can't buy, only live through.

Trenuci koji
oblikuju atmosferu. Moments that
shape the atmosphere.

Na treatme.life retreatu nema popisa aktivnosti koje trebaš odraditi. Postoje prakse, obroci i popodneva koja su u potpunosti tvoja, i između tih stvari događa se ono što je teže nazvati, ali lakše prepoznati. At a treatme.life retreat there's no list of activities to get through. There are practices, meals, and afternoons that are entirely yours, and in between those things, something happens that's harder to name but easier to recognise.

Svaki retreat je malo drugačiji, Istra i Maroko imaju različitu atmosferu, svaka sezona nosi svoju boju, a ritam grupe uvijek pomakne stvari na mjesta koja nisi mogao/la predvidjeti. To je dio onoga što ga čini pravim iskustvom. Each retreat is a little different, Istria and Morocco have their own distinct atmospheres, every season carries its own character, and the group's rhythm always shifts things in directions you couldn't have anticipated. That's part of what makes it a real experience.

01 · Pokret 01 · Movement

Dvije prakse dnevno.
Nikad iste. Two practices daily.
Never the same.

Jutarnja praksa s Petrom počinje u tišini, u tijelu, ne u glavi. Hatha, Ashtanga ili Yin, ovisno o danu, grupi i onome što je u zraku. Nije to sat koji se odvija po rasporedu, više nalik istraživanju nego satnici. Morning practice with Petra begins in quiet, in the body, not the mind. Hatha, Ashtanga or Yin, depending on the day, the group, and what's in the air. It's not a class that follows a syllabus, closer to an inquiry than a schedule.

Večernja je sporija. Mekša. Daje prostora onome što se skupilo kroz dan. Obje su za sve razine, nema natjecanja, nema usporedbe. Samo ti i ono što tijelo već zna, ali rijetko sluša. The evening practice is slower. Softer. It makes space for what has gathered through the day. Both are for all levels, no competition, no comparison. Just you and what the body already knows but rarely gets to listen to.

02 · Hrana i stol 02 · Food & table

Tri obroka.
Svaki od njih je bitan. Three meals.
Every one of them matters.

Hrana ovdje prati ritam - svježina jutra, toplina podneva, opuštenost večeri. Salemova kuhinja, na retreatima kojima se pridružuje, donosi Maroko na tanjur: začini koji ostaju u zraku dugo nakon jela, darežljivost koja ne pita hoćeš li još, tišina oko stola dok se sve slježe. Food here follows the rhythm, the freshness of morning, the warmth of midday, the ease of evening. Salem's kitchen, on the retreats he joins, brings Morocco to the plate: spices that linger in the air long after eating, a generosity that doesn't ask if you want more, the quiet around the table while everything settles.

Kad Salem nije s nama, Ines nastavlja tu nit, isti recepti, isti pristup, ista briga. Uvijek biljno. Uvijek darežljivo. Nitko ne ustaje od stola gladan/gladna. When Salem isn't with us, Ines carries the thread, same recipes, same approach, same care. Always plant-based. Always generous. Nobody leaves the table wanting.

03 · Maroko 03 · Morocco

Dolazi
prije nego što stigneš. It arrives
before you do.

Toplina u zraku ima svoju gustoću, drugačiju od Europe. Jutro na Atlantiku je bijelo i mirno. Salem je već u kuhinji. Miris koji probija kroz prozor nije kava, to je kim i korijander koji se polako bude. The warmth in the air has its own density, different from Europe. A morning on the Atlantic is white and quiet. Salem is already in the kitchen. The smell coming through the window isn't coffee, it's cumin and coriander slowly waking up.

Neke stvari u Maroku ne možeš planirati: spontana posjeta tržnici, čaj koji pita da sjediš, svjetlost pred zalaskom koja mijenja boju svake površine. Salemov teritorij. Maroko kroz ljude koji ga zovu domom. Some things in Morocco can't be planned: a spontaneous market visit, tea that asks you to sit, the light before sunset that changes the colour of every surface. Salem's territory. Morocco through the people who call it home.

04 · Istra 04 · Istria

Sol u zraku
od sedam ujutro. Salt in the air
at seven in the morning.

Istra ima specifičan miris ujutro, more, suho kamenje, rano sunce koje ne grije, samo najavljuje. Jutarnja praksa na terasi s pogledom na Jadran nije dekoracija. Zbog nje neke asane osjećaš drugačije nego kod kuće. Istria has a particular smell in the morning, sea, dry stone, early sun that doesn't yet warm, only announces itself. Morning practice on a terrace overlooking the Adriatic isn't decoration. Because of it, certain postures feel different than they do at home.

Popodneva su tvoja. More, maslinik, hlad. Zajednički stol pod otvorenim nebom. Ritam koji usporava sam od sebe, niko ga ne mora voditi. Ljeto u rujnu koje se ne žuri otići. Afternoons are yours. The sea, an olive grove, shade. A shared table under open sky. A rhythm that slows itself, nobody has to guide it. Summer in September that isn't in any hurry to leave.

Nije uvijek
tjedan dana. Not always
a full week.

Razumijemo da tjedan nije uvijek moguć. Kraći boravak nosi sve ono što je bitno, samo kraće. A privatni retreat za par, prijatelje ili manju grupu nudi potpuno prilagođen ritam, u terminu koji odgovara vama. We understand that a week isn't always possible. A shorter stay carries everything that matters, just compressed. And a private retreat for a couple, friends, or a small group offers a fully tailored rhythm, on dates that work for you.

Kraći boravak Short stay

Tri noći, četiri dana. Ništa nije izuzeto, samo kraće traje. Dvije prakse dnevno, svi obroci, slobodna popodneva. Za one koji znaju što trebaju, ali ne mogu otići cijeli tjedan. Three nights, four days. Nothing is cut, only compressed. Two practices daily, all meals, free afternoons. For those who know what they need but can't step away for a whole week.
