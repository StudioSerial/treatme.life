— Zašto wellness— Why wellness

Gosti dolaze po
iskustvo koje ostaje. Guests come for
the experience that stays.

Bazen, spa i pogled privlače pažnju. Ali ono čega se gosti sjećaju mjesecima nakon povratka kući jest kako su se osjećali. Pool, spa and view attract attention. But what guests remember months after returning home is how they felt.

Upravo zato suvremeni wellness više nije dodatna usluga, već dio cjelokupnog doživljaja hotela. Programi koji stvaraju povezanost, prisutnost i autentične trenutke postaju razlog dolaska, i razlog povratka. That's why modern wellness is no longer an add-on service, but part of the hotel's overall experience. Programmes that create connection, presence and authentic moments become the reason for coming, and for returning.

01

Wellness kao razlog odabira. Wellness as the reason they choose you.

Današnji gosti sve češće biraju hotele koji nude više od smještaja. Traže iskustva koja podržavaju ravnotežu, zdravlje i kvalitetu boravka. Dobro osmišljen wellness program ne obogaćuje samo ponudu hotela, on stvara vrijednost koju gosti prepoznaju i pamte. Today's guests increasingly choose hotels that offer more than accommodation. They seek experiences that support balance, health and quality of stay. A well-designed wellness programme doesn't just enrich the hotel's offer, it creates value that guests recognise and remember.

02

Diferenciacija od iste ponude. Stand out from identical offers.

Bazen, spa i izleti su na popisu svakog hotela na Jadranu. Potpis programa s prepoznatljivim instruktorom i autorskim formatima mijenja razgovor koji se vodi o vašem hotelu, online i usmeno. Pool, spa and excursions are on every Adriatic hotel's list. A signature programme with a recognisable instructor and original formats changes the conversation about your hotel, online and by word of mouth.

03

Organski marketing content. Organic marketing content.

Jutarnji sunrise flow na terasi, Salemov plant breakfast, zajednički obrok, gosti to fotografiraju i dijele bez da ih itko zamoli. Editorial fotografija ostaje hotelu i koristi se godinama. A morning sunrise flow on the terrace, Salem's plant breakfast, a shared meal, guests photograph and share it without anyone asking. Editorial photography stays with the hotel and is used for years.

04

Povrat gosta i viši ADR. Guest return and higher ADR.

Hoteli koji nude autentičan wellbeing program bilježe povrat gosta od 34% više u odnosu na prosječan lifestyle hotel. Specifičan razlog za dolazak opravdava i višu sobarinu, gost ne gleda samo na cijenu, nego na vrijednost ukupnog iskustva. Hotels offering authentic wellbeing programmes see 34% higher guest return rates versus average lifestyle hotels. A specific reason to visit also justifies higher room rates, the guest isn't just comparing price, but total experience value.
