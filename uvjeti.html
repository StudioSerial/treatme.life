01

Rezervacija i pologBooking and deposit

Rezervacija mjesta na retreatu postaje potvrđena po primitku pologa od €200 po osobi. Do tada se mjesto ne drži rezerviranim. A retreat booking is confirmed upon receipt of a €200 deposit per person. Until then the spot is not held.

Puni iznos dospijeva 30 dana prije početka retreata. Faktura se šalje automatski 35 dana prije. Ako puni iznos nije plaćen do 30 dana prije, zadržavamo pravo ponuditi mjesto drugom polazniku. The full amount is due 30 days before the retreat starts. An invoice is sent automatically 35 days in advance. If the full amount isn't paid by 30 days before, we reserve the right to offer the spot to another participant.

Plaćanje je moguće bankovnim transferom (IBAN na fakturi) ili karticom putem linka koji šaljemo uz potvrdu rezervacije. Payment is possible by bank transfer (IBAN on invoice) or card via a link we send with the booking confirmation.

02

Otkazivanje od strane polaznikaCancellation by the guest

Otkazivanje je potrebno prijaviti pisanim putem na hello@treatme.life. Datum primitka emaila je mjerodavan za izračun roka. Cancellations must be submitted in writing to hello@treatme.life. The date the email is received is the reference date for calculating the deadline.

Prijenos mjesta: ako otkažeš manje od 30 dana prije, možeš prenijeti rezervaciju na drugu osobu ili sljedeći termin uz pismeni dogovor i eventualnu razliku u cijeni. Prijenos je moguć do 12 dana prije početka retreata. Transfer option: if you cancel less than 30 days before, you can transfer your booking to another person or a future retreat by written agreement and any price difference. Transfers are possible up to 12 days before the retreat starts.

03

Otkazivanje od strane treatme.lifeCancellation by treatme.life

U slučaju da treatme.life otkaže retreat (viša sila, nedovoljan broj polaznika ili drugi opravdani razlog), svim polaznicima se vraća puni plaćeni iznos u roku od 14 dana. If treatme.life cancels a retreat (force majeure, insufficient participants or other justified reason), all participants receive a full refund of the amount paid within 14 days.

treatme.life ne odgovara za troškove putovanja, smještaja izvan retreata, viza, putnog osiguranja ili drugih troškova koje je polaznik imao u vezi s planiranim retreatom. treatme.life is not liable for travel costs, accommodation outside the retreat, visas, travel insurance or other costs the participant incurred in connection with the planned retreat.

Preporučamo putno osiguranje koje pokriva otkazivanje putovanja — posebno za letove i dulja putovanja do retreata u Maroku. We recommend travel insurance that covers trip cancellation — especially for flights and longer journeys to the Morocco retreat.

06

Zdravlje i odgovornostHealth and liability

Polaznici potvrđuju prijavom da su u dobroj fizičkoj i mentalnoj kondiciji za sudjelovanje u yoga retreatu. Ako postoje zdravstvena stanja koja bi mogla utjecati na praksu, molimo da nas kontaktiraš unaprijed. By booking, participants confirm they are in good physical and mental condition for participation in a yoga retreat. If there are health conditions that could affect practice, please contact us in advance.

Trudnoća: Yin i restorativni formati su dostupni uz prethodnu najavu. Dinamičniji formati (Hatha, Ashtanga) nisu preporučeni u prvom tromjesečju. Kontaktiraj nas za individualni razgovor. Pregnancy: Yin and restorative formats are available with prior notice. More dynamic formats (Hatha, Ashtanga) are not recommended in the first trimester. Contact us for an individual conversation.

treatme.life nije terapeutski program i ne zamjenjuje medicinsku ili psihološku skrb. Sudjelovanje je na vlastitu odgovornost polaznika. treatme.life ne odgovara za ozljede nastale nepoštivanjem uputa učitelja. treatme.life is not a therapeutic programme and does not replace medical or psychological care. Participation is at the participant's own risk. treatme.life is not liable for injuries resulting from not following teacher instructions.

07

Privatnost i fotografijaPrivacy and photography

Na retreatu nastaju fotografije za brand materijale treatme.life. Ne fotografiramo goste bez dozvole. Ako želiš sudjelovati na fotografijama, to naznačiš pri prijavi. Dozvola za objavu fotografija traži se posebno i pisano. During retreats, photographs are taken for treatme.life brand materials. We do not photograph guests without consent. If you wish to appear in photographs, you indicate this at sign-up. Permission to publish photographs is requested separately and in writing.

Vaši osobni podaci (ime, email, kontakt) koriste se isključivo za organizaciju retreata i ne dijele se s trećim stranama. Podaci se čuvaju prema važećem zakonodavstvu EU o zaštiti osobnih podataka (GDPR). Your personal data (name, email, contact) is used solely for retreat organisation and is not shared with third parties. Data is stored in accordance with applicable EU personal data protection legislation (GDPR).
