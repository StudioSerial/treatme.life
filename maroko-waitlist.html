— kakav je taj retreat — what the retreat is like

Nismo sigurni
da možemo opisati
Maroko riječima. We're not sure we can
describe Morocco
in words.

Edition I bila je naša prva Istra — i vidjeli smo što se dogodi kad mala grupa provede tjedan dana u provjerenoj kući, s hranom koja zaista hrani i yogom koja nije nastup. Nešto se promijeni. Tiho. Salem je to opisao kao "jesti iz sjećanja." Edition I was our first Istra — and we saw what happens when a small group spends a week in a trusted house, with food that truly nourishes and yoga that isn't a performance. Something shifts. Quietly. Salem described it as "eating from memory."

Maroko je sljedeći korak. Drugačiji kraj. Ista pažnja. Planine, miris začina, tiha kuća negdje daleko od turizma — i isti tim koji radi s razlogom, ne po scenariju. Morocco is the next step. A different part of the world. The same care. Mountains, the scent of spice, a quiet house somewhere far from tourism — and the same team that works with intention, not from a script.
