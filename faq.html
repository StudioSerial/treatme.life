01

RetreatiRetreats

Za ljude koji žele više od odmorišta — koji traže prostor za usporavanje, svjestan pokret i hranu s pažnjom. Nije za one koji traže party atmosferu, intenzivne detoks programe ili retreat ispunjen aktivnostima. Dobrodošli su početnici i iskusni praktičari, solo putnici i parovi. Zajednički nazivnik: želja za tišinom i namjerom. For people who want more than a holiday — who are looking for space to slow down, conscious movement and mindful food. Not for those seeking party vibes, intense detox programmes or activity-packed schedules. Beginners and experienced practitioners are welcome, solo travellers and couples alike. Common ground: a desire for stillness and intention.
Rabac je malo ribarsko mjesto na istočnoj obali Istre, cca 10 km od Labina. Najbliža je zračna luka Pula (oko 60 km) ili Rijeka (oko 80 km). Organiziramo transfer s/do dogovorene točke preuzimanja — informacije dajemo po potvrdi rezervacije. Vlastiti auto nije neophodan. Rabac is a small fishing village on the eastern Istrian coast, about 10 km from Labin. The nearest airports are Pula (approx. 60 km) or Rijeka (approx. 80 km). We organise a transfer to/from an agreed pick-up point — details are shared after booking confirmation. A car is not necessary.
Edition I i II u Istri traju 5 noći (dolazak utorkom, odlazak nedjeljom). Retreat u Maroku traje 7 noći. Točni datumi su vidljivi na stranici svakog retreata. Edition I and II in Istria last 5 nights (arrival Tuesday, departure Sunday). The Morocco retreat is 7 nights. Exact dates are shown on each retreat's page.
Uključeno: smještaj u Vurotu, sva hrana za vrijeme retreata (Salemova plant-based kuhinja), svi yoga satovi i radionice, materijali (prostirka, blokovi, remen), te transfer s/do dogovorene točke preuzimanja. Nije uključeno: putni troškovi, osobni troškovi i putno osiguranje. Included: accommodation at Vurot, all meals during the retreat (Salem's plant-based kitchen), all yoga sessions and workshops, props (mat, blocks, strap), and transfer to/from the agreed pick-up point. Not included: travel costs, personal expenses and travel insurance.
Da, svi retreati su open-level. Petra prilagođava svaki sat individualnim potrebama. Nema natjecanja, nema očekivanja. Jedini uvjet je volja za istraživanjem vlastite prakse. Yes, all retreats are open-level. Petra adapts each session to individual needs. No competition, no expectations. The only requirement is a willingness to explore your own practice.
Ne, prostirke, blokovi i remeni su osigurani. Ako imaš vlastitu prostirku i preferiraš ju koristiti, slobodno donesi. No, mats, blocks and straps are provided. If you have your own mat and prefer to use it, feel free to bring it.
Maksimalno 8 gostiju po retreatu. To nije kapacitetno ograničenje, to je svjesna odluka. Mali format omogućuje stvarni kontakt, individualnu pažnju i autentičnu skupnu dinamiku koja nestaje čim povećaš broj. A maximum of 8 guests per retreat. This isn't a capacity limit, it's a deliberate decision. The small format allows real connection, individual attention and authentic group dynamics that disappear once you scale up.

30–60 days: 50% refund.

HranaFood

Plant-based, ali nije striktno vegan. Salem koristi lokalni med i ponekad svježu ribu s istarskog tržišta. Hrana je bez glutena ondje gdje je moguće. Uvijek pitamo za alergije i prehrambene restrikcije pri prijavi. Plant-based, but not strictly vegan. Salem uses local honey and occasionally fresh fish from the Istrian market. Food is gluten-free where possible. We always ask about allergies and dietary restrictions at sign-up.
Kontaktiraj nas unaprijed na hello@treatme.life. Salem prilagođava jelovnik svakom gostu, nismo restoran s fiksnim menijima, pa je prilagodba stvarna, a ne kompromis. Contact us in advance at hello@treatme.life. Salem adapts the menu to each guest, we're not a restaurant with fixed menus, so adaptations are genuine, not compromises.
Hrana je sastavni dio programa i nije samo "prehrana", to su zajednički obroci s namjerom. Slobodni si, naravno, ali preporučamo da ostaneš u ritmu retreata jer to pojačava ukupno iskustvo. Food is part of the programme, not just "catering", these are communal meals with intention. You're free to eat outside, of course, but we recommend staying in the retreat rhythm as it deepens the overall experience.

SmještajAccommodation

Bez WiFi-a u sobama, dostupan u zajedničkim prostorijama. Kupaonica je dijeljena. A stone house from 1912, restored with care for original materials. Twin/double rooms (shared with one other person) and one single room (available on request). No WiFi in rooms, available in common areas. Bathroom is shared.
Da, i preporučamo. Većina gostiju dolazi solo. Mali format (max 8 osoba) znači da se brzo povežeš s ostalima, i da nisi nikad sam/sama u praznini. Udio solo gostiju na našim retreatima je oko 70%. Yes, and we recommend it. The majority of guests come solo. The small format (max 8 people) means you connect quickly with others, and you're never alone in a void. Around 70% of our retreat guests come solo.
Nema WiFi-a u sobama, to je svjesna odluka, ne tehnički nedostatak. WiFi je dostupan u zajedničkom prostoru za nužne potrebe. Retreat je dizajniran kao pauza od ekrana, a ne bijeg od stvarnosti. No WiFi in rooms, that's a deliberate choice, not a technical gap. WiFi is available in the common area for essential needs. The retreat is designed as a pause from screens, not a flight from reality.

Comfortable yoga clothes (a few sets, since it's a daily practice), light summer wear, a hat and sun cream, personal toiletries, a book if you like reading, and an open mind. A mat, blocks and strap are provided. Leave the laptop at home — no WiFi in rooms, and that's intentional.
