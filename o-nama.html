Treat Me Life nije nastao iz poslovnog plana. Nastao je iz godina zajedničkih obroka, dugih razgovora i prijateljstva koje tiho oblikuje sve. Treat Me Life didn't begin with a business plan. It began with years of shared meals, long conversations, and the kind of friendship that quietly shapes everything.

Petra i Ines poznaju se od djetinjstva. Godinama su kretale vlastitim putevima, no nešto ih je uvijek vraćalo jedno prema drugom. Kad je trenutak došao, stvaranje nečeg istinskog činilo se jedinom mogućnošću. Petra and Ines have known each other since childhood. Over the years, they followed their own paths, but something kept pulling their worlds back together. When the moment came, creating something real felt like the only logical thing to do.

Ono što nudimo nije program. To je poziv, da se krećeš, odmoriš, doživiš nešto izvanredno i sjetiš se zašto biti prisutna tako dobro doći. What we offer is not a program. It's an invitation, to move, to rest, to eat something extraordinary, and to remember why being present feels so good.

Posljednji dio složio se u Maroku. The last piece arrived in Morocco.

Petra i Ines odrasle su zajedno, a potom svaka krenula svojim putem, vlastitim otkrićima, vlastitom verzijom svijeta. Ali život ima način da prave ljude vrati jedno drugom. Petra and Ines grew up together, then apart, each following her own path, her own discoveries, her own version of the world. But life has a way of weaving the right people back to each other.

Petra je neko vrijeme živjela u Maroku, poučavajući yogu, živeći polako, kad je upoznala Salema. Čovjeka rođenog i odraslog u pustinji, koji kuha onako kako se pustinja osjeća: darežljivo, bez žurbe, duboko. Njegova hrana nije bila usluga. Bila je oblik brige. Petra was spending time in Morocco, teaching yoga, living slowly, when she met Salem. A man born and raised in the desert, who cooks the way the desert feels: generous, unhurried, deep. His food wasn't a service. It was a form of care.

U tom trenutku Treat Me Life je postao cjelovit. Tri različita svijeta, jedan zajednički ritam. That was the moment Treat Me Life became whole. Three different worlds, one shared rhythm.

— Što nas vodi— What guides us

Četiri stvari
u koje vjerujemo. Four things
we believe in.

01

Malo je dovoljno.Small is enough.

Mali retreati. Mali satovi. Namjerno ograničena mjesta, ne jer ne možemo više, nego jer više uništava ono što tražimo. Small retreats. Small classes. Intentionally limited spots, not because we can't do more, but because more destroys what we're after.

02

Pokret koji
ostavlja traga.Movement that
stays with you.

Ne vježbamo da bismo izgledali. Vježbamo da bismo osjetili. Tijelo kao prostor istraživanja, ne postignuća. We don't practice to perform. We practice to feel. The body as a space for inquiry, not achievement.

03

Hrana nije
dodatak.Food is not
a supplement.

Salem kuha s retreatom, ne za retreat. Što jedemo, kad jedemo i s kim jedemo, jednako je važno kao što radimo na prostirci. Kad Salem nije s nama, Ines prenosi tu istu pažnju i brigu. Salem cooks with the retreat, not for it. What we eat, when we eat, and who we eat with, matters as much as what we do on the mat. When Salem isn't with us, Ines carries that same care forward.

04

Autentičnost
nije branding.Authenticity
isn't branding.

Ne pretvaramo se da imamo sve odgovore. Ovaj projekt živi jer ga živimo, svaki dan, i dalje. We don't pretend to have all the answers. This project lives because we live it, every day, still figuring it out.

Yoga · Maroko · Hrvatska Yoga · Morocco · Croatia

Petra

Suosnivačica · Yoga učiteljica Co-founder · Yoga Teacher

Deset godina poučavanja. Cijeli život u tijelu. Petrin odnos s pokretom počeo je u djetinjstvu, klasičnim baletom, a potom yogom koju joj je pokazao otac. Tek u kasnim dvadesetima njezina je praksa prešla granice oblika i ušla u nešto dublje: dah, osjete, tijelo kao prostor istinskog istraživanja.

Godinama je poučavala na Gozu i Malti, a potom dvije godine kao rezidentna učiteljica u Fairmont Spa Resortu u Maroku. Danas živi između Maroka i Hrvatske, dijeleći Hatha yogu, Yin, somatski i utjelovljeni pokret.

Njena nastava je utemeljujuća, intuitivna i duboko osobna. Ne vodi samo kroz asane, daje učenicima znatiželju i alate da pronađu vlastitu praksu. Ten years of teaching. A lifetime in the body. Petra's relationship with movement began in childhood, classical ballet, then yoga introduced by her father. It wasn't until her late twenties that her practice shifted from shapes into something deeper: breath, sensation, the body as a place of genuine inquiry.

She spent years teaching in Gozo and Malta, then two years as resident teacher at the Fairmont Spa Resort in Morocco. Today she moves between Morocco and Croatia, sharing traditional Hatha, Yin, somatic and embodied movement.

Her teaching is grounding, intuitive, and deeply personal. She doesn't just guide postures, she gives her students the curiosity and the tools to find their own practice.

Brand · Biznis · Iskustvo Brand · Business · Experience

Ines

Suosnivačica · Brand & Poslovni razvoj Co-founder · Brand & Business Development

Ines je ona koja drži sve zajedno, i ono što se vidi, i ono što ostaje iza kulisa.

Izgradila je brand od temelja: vizualni identitet, komunikacija, poslovni razvoj, partnerstva. Vodi operativnu stvarnost svakog retreata, logistiku, ugovore, iskustvo gostiju, svakodnevni tijek, sve ono što cijelu stvar zapravo čini mogućom.

Njezin pristup je miran, temeljit i tiho odan detaljima. Jednako pažljivo promišlja estetiku stola i uvjete poslovnog sporazuma.

I kad Salem nije s nama, preuzima kuhinju, prateći njegove recepte, njegovu pažnju, njegov pristup. Hrana se malo mijenja sa sezonom. Osjećaj ne. Ines is the one who holds everything together, both what you see, and what stays behind the scenes.

She built the brand from the ground up: visual identity, communications, business development, partnerships. She manages the operational reality of every retreat, logistics, contracts, guest experience, daily flow, everything that makes the whole thing actually work.

Her approach is calm, thorough, and quietly devoted to detail. She thinks with equal care about the aesthetics of a table setting and the terms of a business agreement.

And when Salem isn't with us, she tends the kitchen too, following his recipes, his approach, his care. The food changes slightly with the season. The feeling doesn't.

Gostujući kuhar · Maroko Guest Chef · Morocco

Salem

Gostujući kuhar Guest Chef

Salem je rođen i odrastao u marokanskoj pustinji. Kuha onako kako je odrastao, strpljivo, darežljivo, s instinktom za ono što je ljudima zaista potrebno.

Njegova hrana je tradicionalna i duboko hranjiva. Jela izgrađena na sjećanjima, začinima i toplini koju tanjur zapravo može nositi.

Salem se pridružuje odabranim retreatima kroz godinu. Kad stigne, kuhinja postaje svojevrsna ceremonija, a stol, pravo mjesto okupljanja. Salem was born and raised in the Moroccan desert. He cooks the way he was raised, with patience, generosity, and an instinct for what people truly need.

His food is traditional and deeply nourishing. Dishes built on memory, spice, and the kind of warmth that a plate of food can actually carry.

Salem joins selected retreats throughout the year. When he arrives, the kitchen becomes its own kind of ceremony, and the table, its own kind of gathering.

Salemova kuhinja ukorijenjena je u marokansku tradiciju, spora, darežljiva i duboko živa. Jela koja hrane ne samo tijelo, nego i nešto tiše ispod njega. Kad se pridruži retreatu, kuhinja nosi vlastitu energiju, a stol postaje mjesto gdje se događa nešto istinsko. Salem's cooking is rooted in Moroccan tradition, slow, generous, and deeply alive. Dishes that nourish not just the body but something quieter underneath. When he joins a retreat, the kitchen carries its own energy, and the table becomes a place where something real happens.

Kad Salem nije s nama, Ines preuzima kuhinju, prateći njegove recepte, njegov pristup, njegovu pažnju. Hrana se može malo promijeniti sa sezonom, ali osjećaj ostaje isti: obilje, utjeha, kuhano s punom pažnjom. When Salem isn't with us, Ines tends the kitchen, following his recipes, his approach, his care. The food may shift slightly with the season, but the feeling stays the same: abundant, comforting, made with full attention.
