Tko smoWho we are

treatme.life je wellness i retreat brand. Operativno se vodi kroz kontakt adresu hello@treatme.life. Ova pravila opisuju kako prikupljamo i koristimo osobne podatke posjetitelja ovog web-mjesta.

treatme.life is a wellness and retreat brand. Operated through the contact address hello@treatme.life. This policy describes how we collect and use personal data from visitors to this website.

Koji podatci se prikupljajuWhat data we collect

Prikupljamo samo podatke koje nam dobrovoljno pružiš:

We only collect data you voluntarily provide to us:

  • Email adresa — kada se prijaviš na naš newsletter ili pošalješ upit.
  • Email address — when you subscribe to our newsletter or submit an enquiry.
  • Ime i prezime — kada pošalješ rezervacijski upit za retreat.
  • Name and surname — when you submit a retreat booking enquiry.
  • Poruka — sadržaj upita koji pošalješ putem emaila.
  • Message content — the content of any enquiry you send via email.

Kako koristimo tvoje podatkeHow we use your data

  • Slanje newslettera (jednom mjesečno, bez spama)
  • Sending our newsletter (once a month, no spam)
  • Odgovaranje na rezervacijske upite i komunikacija vezana uz retreat
  • Responding to booking enquiries and retreat-related communication
  • Poboljšanje naše usluge i sadržaja
  • Improving our service and content

Nikad ne prodajemo, ne iznajmljujemo niti dijelimo tvoje podatke s trećim stranama bez tvoje privole.

We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties without your consent.

As an EU resident, you have the following rights: access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, and objection. For all requests, write to hello@treatme.life.
