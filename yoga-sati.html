Online sati Online classes

Prisutnost ne
zahtijeva prostor. Presence doesn't
require a room.

Ne predajem grupi, vidim svakoga posebno. Male grupe znače da te zaista mogu pratiti, prilagoditi upute, sugerirati nešto specifično tebi. Nema snimke, nema anonimnosti. Svaki sat je živ i oblikuje se prema onima koji su tu taj dan. I don't teach to the group, I see each person individually. Small groups mean I can really follow you, adapt the guidance, offer something specific to you. No recording, no anonymity. Every class is alive and shapes itself around whoever is there that day.

Petra
Yoga · Hatha · Yin · Privatni satovi Yoga · Hatha · Yin · Private sessions

Put s pokretom počeo je klasičnim baletom, a otac ju je uveo u yogu. U kasnim dvadesetima praksa je postala istraživanje tijela, emocija, daha. Poučavala na Gozu i Malti, dvije godine rezidentna učiteljica u Fairmont Spa Resortu, Maroko. Danas živi između Maroka i Hrvatske. Movement began with classical ballet; her father introduced her to yoga. In her late twenties practice became an exploration, of body, emotion, breath. She taught on Gozo and Malta, then two years as resident teacher at the Fairmont Spa Resort, Morocco. Today she lives between Morocco and Croatia.

10+ godina prakse · Certificirana hatha instruktorica 10+ years practice · Certified hatha instructor

Ritriti · Komunikacija · Mjesta Retreats · Communication · Places
