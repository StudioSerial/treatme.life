O retreatuAbout the retreat

Što je ovo, zapravo? What this actually is.

treatme.life retreati nisu wellness program. Nema rasporeda aktivnosti, nema obaveznih radionica, nema grupnih razgovora o osjetljivim temama. Postoje dvije yoga prakse, dobra hrana i prostor za sve ostalo. treatme.life retreats are not wellness programmes. There's no activity schedule, no obligatory workshops, no group conversations about sensitive topics. There are two yoga practices, good food and space for everything else.

More je odmah tu. Jutro počinje yogom, dan završava tišinom. Između toga, plivanje, knjige, razgovori koji se dogode sami od sebe, ili apsolutno ništa. Svaki gost sam određuje kako provodi slobodno vrijeme. The sea is right there. The morning starts with yoga, the day ends in silence. Between that, swimming, books, conversations that happen on their own, or absolutely nothing. Each guest decides how to spend their free time.

Maksimalno 8 gostiju. Petra zna sve po imenu, Salem zna što voliš jesti, Ines zna kad ti treba prostor i kad ti treba razgovor. To je jedina obećana transformacija, osjećaj da te netko vidi i čuje. Maximum 8 guests. Petra knows everyone by name, Salem knows what you like to eat, Ines knows when you need space and when you need conversation. That's the only promised transformation, the feeling of being seen and heard.

FAQ

Pitanja o ovom retreatu. Questions about this retreat.

Kako doći do Rabca?How to get to Rabac?
Rabac je ~150 km od Zagreba i ~90 km od Rijeke - okvirno 2.5 sata autom. Najbliži aerodrom je Pula (~50 km). Za one bez auta, organiziramo zajednički prijevoz ako ima dovoljan interes. Javi nam pri rezervaciji.Rabac is ~150 km from Zagreb and ~90 km from Rijeka, roughly 2.5 hours by car. Nearest airport is Pula (~50 km). For those without a car, we organise shared transport if there's sufficient interest. Let us know at booking.
Moram li znati plivati?Do I need to know how to swim?
Ne. Rabac ima miran zaljev i plitka mjesta. Plivanje nije obavezno, ideš u more jer je lijepo, ali možeš mirno sjediti na obali. Nitko te neće gledati s očekivanjem da skočiš.No. The Adriatic near Rabac has a calm bay and shallow spots. Swimming isn't mandatory, you go in the water because it's beautiful, but you can sit quietly on the shore. Nobody will watch with expectations that you jump in.
Je li retreat prikladan za muškarce?Is the retreat suitable for men?
Da, apsolutno. Otprilike trećina gostiju na prethodnim retreatima bili su muškarci. Yoga nije "ženski sport" i mi ne gradimo retreat oko rodni estetike, gradimo ga oko tišine, hrane i pokreta. To je za svakoga tko treba pauzirati.Yes, absolutely. Roughly a third of guests at previous retreats were men. Yoga isn't a "women's sport" and we don't build the retreat around gender aesthetics, we build it around silence, food and movement. That's for anyone who needs to pause.
Kakav smještaj čeka me u kući?What accommodation is in the house?
GreenTree Villa ima 4 sobe: 2 dvokrevetne (za dijeljeni smještaj) i 2 jednokrevetne (privatni smještaj). Sve sobe imaju prirodno svjetlo, pogled na zelenilo ili more. Zajedničke prostorije uključuju veliku kuhinju, dnevni boravak i terasu s pogledom na Jadran.GreenTree Villa has 4 rooms, 2 double rooms (for shared accommodation) and 2 single rooms (private accommodation). All rooms have natural light, with views of greenery or the sea. Shared spaces include a large kitchen, living room and terrace with Adriatic views.
Trebam li iskustvo s yogom?Do I need yoga experience?
Ne. Retreat je otvoren za sve razine — od potpunih početnika do iskusnih praktikanata. Svaka jutarnja praksa nudi prilagodbe i alternative, a Petra vodi grupu s pažnjom prema različitim potrebama. Najvažnije je da dolaziš otvorena/otvoren, ne da dolaziš spreman/a.No. The retreat is open to all levels — complete beginners through experienced practitioners. Every morning practice offers modifications and alternatives, and Petra leads the group with attention to individual needs. What matters most is that you arrive open, not that you arrive ready.
Mogu li doći sama/sam?Can I come alone?
Da — i većina gostiju dolazi upravo tako. Mnogi dolaze sami jer im treba prostor daleko od svakodnevne dinamike, a upravo to taj format nudi. U grupi od 8 osoba prirodno se stvori bliskost, bez da moraš biti društven ako ne želiš. Nema awkward ice-breakera. Nema obaveze razgovora.Yes — and most guests do exactly that. Many come alone because they need space away from everyday dynamics, and that's precisely what this format offers. In a group of 8, closeness forms naturally, without you having to be social if you don't want to. No awkward ice-breakers. No obligation to talk.

Polog od €500 rezervira tvoje mjesto i potvrđuje rezervaciju. Ostatak od €1.000 plaća se do 22. kolovoza 2026 (30 dana prije retreata). Polog je u potpunosti povratljiv ako otkažeš 45 ili više dana prije početka. Između 44 i 30 dana polog zadržavamo, no ostatak vratimo. The remaining €1,000 is due by 22 August 2026 (30 days before retreat). The deposit is fully refundable if you cancel 45 or more days in advance. Between 44–30 days we retain the deposit but refund the balance. Within 30 days there is no refund — but you can transfer the booking to another person. If TreatMe cancels, a full refund is guaranteed.
Što je uključeno u cijenu?What's included in the price?
Uključeno: 5 noćenja u GreenTree Villi, sve jutarnje prakse (yoga, meditacija), svi obroci (doručak, ručak, večera), Salemova večera za stol, materijali za praksu. Nije uključeno: prijevoz do Rabca, tretmani (masaža, terapije), alkohol i posebne narudžbe, osobni troškovi. Više detalja u sekciji "Što je uključeno" gore.Included: 5 nights at GreenTree Villa, all morning practices (yoga, meditation), all meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner), Salem's dinner at the table, practice materials. Not included: transport to Rabac, treatments (massage, therapies), alcohol and special orders, personal expenses. More detail in the "What's included" section above.
Koliko je gostiju na retreatu?How many guests are there?
Maksimalno 8 gostiju. To je namjerna odluka — mali broj znači da Petra zaista može pratiti svaku osobu u praksi, Salem može kuhati onako kako treba, a grupa se može osjećati kao kuća a ne kao tečaj. Mjesta odlaze brzo upravo zbog toga.Maximum 8 guests. This is a deliberate decision — a small number means Petra can genuinely attend to each person in practice, Salem can cook the way it needs to be cooked, and the group can feel like a home rather than a course. Spots go quickly for exactly this reason.
